New Admissions

Being a public school, form one admissions are done by the Ministry of Education. Vacancies are available on competitive basis. Those seeking admissions can apply for a chance at our school by obtaining and filling an application form. Application forms are available from our school and are returned on the date of admission as specified on the application form.

About Us

Butula School has consistently been among the top performers in the KCSE exams. The school has continued to strengthen its performance in this exams and has hence been ranked the best school in Busia County. With our small-sized classes, we have maintained an appropriate teacher-students ratio to maximize on student learning. With our committed teachers, focused students and supportive parents & all operating as a single team, Butula school will continue to be on the upward trend in academics.
