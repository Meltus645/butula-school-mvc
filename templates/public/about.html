Vision

To be an academic centre of excellence committed to education and development.

Mission

To nurture and cherish the growth of knowledge, skills and values in individuals in order to develop their potentials, promote self actualization and to make them well integrated members of the society.

Achievements

Academics

Butula School has consistently been among the top performers in the KCSE exams. The school has continued to strengthen its performance in this exams and has hence been ranked the best school in Busia County. With our small-sized classes, we have maintained an appropriate teacher-students ratio to maximize on student learning. With our committed teachers, focused students and supportive parents & all operating as a single team, Butula school will continue to be on the upward trend in academics.

Co-Curricular

We are glad as a school that we have managed to discover and progressively grow our students talents. We are bold at this because our students have always outshined our participants in various co-curricular activities frequently reaching the national level in our engagements. These engagements include: Sports, Debates, Drama Festivals, Music and so on.

Facilities

Butula school boasts of elegant infrastructure designed to give the school its national status feel. We have an ultra-modern extensive and well equipped science laboratory, a full stocked, active, ICT driven and extensive research center,the library, 28 air conditioned class rooms, a modern dining hall, a beckery, one football field, a bascket ball court two indoor games courts and modern dormitories.
